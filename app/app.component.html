Bloop Ball

A game of wit, creativity and agility

Origins

  • One

Setup

  • One (7-10') x (4-6') rug
  • Room of minimum 15' x 10'
  • Rubber ball, 1/2 inflation
  • 2 or 4 players

Rules

  • The rug serves as the 'net', the point of transfer from one player to another.
  • Reminiscent of table tennis, the serve must bounce on either player's side.
  • To serve toss the ball with your left hand and strike the ball with one fluent motion.
  • some part of your body other than your arms (in this case similar to soccer).
  • You are only permitted to use your hand once per possesion.
  • You may only allow the ball to bounce once on the surface to your side of the rug.
  • inbounds.
  • A player may strike the ball while in the air so long as they last set foot on their side of the ends of the rug. They may not land on any part of the rug if that is the last touch of possesion.

Any questions? Check out our instructional video to see if those questions of yours may be clarified.
